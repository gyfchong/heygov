Edinburgh Parks Industrial Estate

Organisation Renewal SA

Covering 600 hectares and close to major transport infrastructure, Edinburgh Parks is one of South Australia’s premier locations for business, industry and manufacturing. It is located 25 km north of the Adelaide CBD between the Elizabeth and Salisbury city centres, and near Port Wakefield Road and the Northern Expressway.

Edinburgh Parks is home to the national headquarters of Australia’s largest defence contractor, BAE Systems, and is adjacent to the RAAF Edinburgh Base and Defence Science and Technology Organisation (DSTO). It also now hosts the 7RAR Battle Group. A wide range of national and international businesses have been attracted to Edinburgh Parks with more than 6,500 jobs created. Major business occupants include Coles, Inghams, MTU Detroit Diesel, DHL,TOLL and Futuris.

About 248 hectares has been sold since the precinct opened in 2005. At 30 June 2013, 36 hectares of development-ready land was available to the market. Allotment sizes range from 2,500 square metres to more than 10 hectares. Marketing of remaining lots in the southern precinct is a focus for 2013-14.

Total project cost is estimated at $1.95 billion with a $100 million State Government contribution and an estimated $1.85 billion from the private sector.
